Server Rules

Play fair, play safe

We keep things simple. Follow these rules and everyone has a great time.

01
Respect Everyone
Treat all players with kindness. Harassment, hate speech, and toxicity will result in a permanent ban.
02
No Griefing
Do not destroy, steal from, or tamper with other players' builds or chests without permission.
03
No Cheating
Hacked clients, x-ray texture packs, and game exploit abuse are strictly forbidden.
04
No Spamming
Keep chat clean and friendly. No advertising, spam, excessive caps, or inappropriate content.
05
Build Thoughtfully
Leave at least 100 blocks of space between your build and others' land unless you have permission.
06
Staff Word is Final
Respect staff decisions. If you disagree, raise a ticket in Discord — don't argue in chat.
